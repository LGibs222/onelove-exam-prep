Good to Know

Using the prep apps

Everything you need to get started

How do I get an access code?

Request one with the form above — it's free during launch. Dr. Gibson will email your code, then you sign in to any app with your name and that code. The same code works across all five apps.

Does my progress save?

Yes. Your pretest results, completed modules, and post-test scores are saved on the device and browser you use, so you can pick up where you left off. Using a different device or clearing your browser starts fresh.

What devices work?

Any modern browser on phone, tablet, or laptop. No install, no account to create — just the access code.

How should I use a course?

Start with the diagnostic pretest to see your weak areas, work the flagged study modules (concept cards, key terms, Quick Checks, sort games, and read-aloud), then take the post-test or mock exam to measure your growth. Flashcards and a quick-quiz mode are there for targeted review.

Is this affiliated with the testing boards?

No. These are independent study aids from OneLove Behavior Analysts, PLLC, built to the publicly available test frameworks. They are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the BACB®, NYSED, or the Evaluation Systems group of Pearson.
